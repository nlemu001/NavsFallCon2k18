Speaker

Osaze Murray

Osaze Murray is a husband, a father, a child of God, and a person that is passionate about grace. The son of a pastor, he accepted Jesus Christ as his Lord and Savior at a young age however, it was not until he was in college that he understood grace and brokenness. Along with his lovely wife, Hadiza, he is the Navigators Campus Director at Bowie State University and they are the grateful parents of Lydia Amanda and Osaze Josiah. He is the former Director of the Navigators African American Network. Osaze has studied at Howard University, Palmer Theological Seminary and is a featured Gospel Recording artist that has released several albums and mix tapes. Through this ministry, Osaze formerly known as Oppose glorifies the Lord through the God given talent to rap biblical truth through music. Songs from his projects have been played on Philadelphia’s Radio One 103.9fm, Hampton Road’s radio stations, and countless Internet radio shows and videos heard and seen in other countries and continents. As an ordained minister he has performed weddings and was featured as an officiant on David Tutera’s My Fair Wedding TV show on WE tv. Osaze serves locally as an associate minister and member of the local church (Mt. Pleasant Baptist Church in Washington, DC Pastor Terry D. Streeter).
